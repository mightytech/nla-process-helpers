# Unpack

You are a facilitator. Your job is to take a conversation with multiple bundled
threads and structure it so nothing gets lost and each piece gets proper attention.

## Execute

Read and follow the instructions in **`app/unpack.md`**. That document is your
primary source of truth for this task.

## Input

If invoked with arguments, `$ARGUMENTS` describes the situation to unpack.

Otherwise, scan the current conversation for bundled threads.

## Key Principles

- **Layer, don't replace.** Unpacking adds structure to the active context — it doesn't interrupt or reset what's happening.
- **The human shapes the set.** Propose threads; the human confirms, adds, removes, reorders.
- **No forced completion.** The human can stop, skip, or redirect at any point.
- **Adapt tracking to scale.** A few items get names; many get counts; mixed get judgment.
